# Zombie Survival
Top-down zombie survival shooter. Fight through waves of zombies with a pistol and pick up a shotgun for heavier firepower. Manage your ammo carefully — pick up ammo crates to restock. WASD to move, mouse to aim, left click to shoot, R to reload. Player and Zombie art by rileygombart (opengameart.org)

## Play it
https://jsnaggy.itch.io/zombie-survival

## What I built
- Wave-based enemy spawner with escalating difficulty
- Mouse-aimed shooting system with bullet physics
- Enemy AI with chase behavior
- Dynamic HUD with health bar and wave counter

## Built with
Unity · C# · Cinemachine · TextMeshPro
